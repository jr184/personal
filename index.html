Welcome! My name is Joshua Rodriguez, and I recently graduated from Humboldt State University with a degree in Environmental Science emphasizing in Geospatial Science. This website consists of my work ranging from cartography, geospatial analysis, interactive maps, and much more. My objective in this field is to mitigate environmental issues spatially, by utilizing ArcMap, ENVI, and most notably programming. Though I am proficient in using ArcMap and ENVI, my talents are best allocated towards web-development, geospatial programming, and cartographic design. I aim to utilize my abilities in hopes of aiding the progression of environmental policies and to further educate and spread awareness of the ever increasing degradation of natural resources through data visualization, whether be it physical or online mapping.
